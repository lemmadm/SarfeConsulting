OUR SERVICES

Here are the list of our various services

Accounting System Set-up

This involves the initial set-up of accounting system for your company which includes the full set of accounting books and records system and accounting policies. This can be done either through cloud computing or manually, depending on your choice

Bookkeeping and Accounting Service

Bookkeeping is the most basic of all accounting functions. Bookkeeping involves maintaining accurate and updated records of all of your company's financial activity. Our methodology includes bank reconciliation, purchase and sale records, and regulatory filings. These records are essential to regulatory compliance. Proper bookkeeping makes other accounting functions, such as audits, payroll, and tax preparation, much simpler and less time consuming.

Financial Analysis

It is important to conduct periodic review and analysis of your company's financial performance. This includes the preparation of financial statements and reporting for internal and external use with focus on management, corporate and statutory requirements. These reviews are an important part of regulatory compliance and shareholder disclosure. Additionally, financial analysis can provide valuable insight into your company's financial health which can help you make sound decisions for the future.

Payroll Accounting

Payroll is a critical business operations function performed by your accounting team. Payroll involves managing all employee compensation, benefits, and withholdings. We manage your payroll on an ongoing basis, ensuring that all employees are compensated according to their employment agreements and benefit selections. This also includes the processing of PAYE Tax for employee excluding the actual tax paid as the actual tax is based.

Tax Preparation

Commercial tax preparation can be extremely complex. Records and submissions must be prepared carefully in order to minimize your risk of unforeseen tax liabilities, fees, or audits. Our team will work with you to prepare complete and accurate corporate tax filings on an annual basis.
